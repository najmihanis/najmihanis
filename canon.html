CANON BUILD
What?
  • Manufactured and machined a small desk toy that shoots bearing balls.
How?
  • Generated G-codes for CNC lathe and mills using Fusion 360.
  • Machined an acrylic block into the body shape using CNC milling.
  • Shaped the cannon barrel with precision CNC lathing.
  • Utilized manual mills, drill presses, and lathe for base plate, uprights, and shooting mechanism fabrication.
  • Cut aluminum sheets with precision using a band saw.
  • Learned and applied safety protocols and shop etiquettes through hands-on practice during the manufacturing process.
