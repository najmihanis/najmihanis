CAPTURING THE MOMENT

The world moves fast, but ink creates a permanent pause. This archive contains little pauses I took in Europe, Yellowstone, San Diego and New York, recording how each city feels while I’m moving through it.

Each sketch was drawn on site with a fountain pen and a small travel watercolor set. No photos, no tracing. Just me, the city, and the time it took to stay present.

TOOLS Lamy Safari / Waterbrush
PAPER 140lb Cold Press
DATE 2024 — 2026
