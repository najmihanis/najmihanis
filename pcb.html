Overview
What
  • Controls a rover science module for sample inspection and spectroscopy.
  • Drives a stepper motor to index a rotating sample wheel with position feedback.
  • Actuates two servos: one for sample interaction and one for light positioning.
  • Switches and powers multiple light sources (LED, UV LED, laser) for fluorescence analysis.
  • Supports optical alignment between light source and spectrometer for color change detection.
How
  • Designed a custom PCB integrating motor control, lighting control, sensing, and power regulation.
  • Used an Arduino Nano as the central controller for motion sequencing and I/O.
  • Integrated a stepper motor driver with a rotary encoder to track sample wheel position.
  • Implemented MOSFET switching to safely control high-current loads (lights, laser, motor power).
  • Designed onboard power regulation to step 12V input down to 7V and 5V for mixed-voltage components.
